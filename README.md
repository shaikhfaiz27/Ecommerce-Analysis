# Project Description
Developed an interactive *E-commerce Business Performance Dashboard* using sales data from 2020–2025 to analyze revenue growth, profit trends, customer behavior, shipping costs, and product performance. The dashboard helps identify business problems such as declining profit margins, high return rates, and regional performance gaps using KPIs, charts, and filters for better decision-making.

* Tracks yearly sales and profit growth
* Identifies high-performing products and categories
* Detects low-profit and loss-making areas
* Analyzes customer purchasing behavior
* Helps reduce shipping and operational costs
* Monitors product return trends
* Improves marketing strategy decisions
* Supports better inventory management
* Compares regional business performance
* Helps management make data-driven decisions
* Tracks target vs actual sales performance
* Improves overall business profitability
